About

A short version of who I am.

I'm Rob. You've probably found me through YouTube, but this site is where everything else lives. The books, the music, the writing, the newsletter, the occasional weird side project.

Quick background. I started out as a welder, talked my way into internet marketing, and somewhere along the line became a YouTuber, author, and music producer. When I'm not making stuff, I'm on a motorcycle or getting punched in the face at muay thai. Keeps things balanced.

YouTube started as a hobby. Something to mess around with on the side. Then the community showed up, and it stopped being a hobby. The friends I've made, the fans who actually give a damn, the people in the comments and the Discord who are just as into this stuff as I am, that's what turned it into a real thing. I'm still kind of blown away by it.

What I do is pretty simple. I find the stories history tried to bury or pretty up, and I tell them straight. No lecture voice. No moralizing. No acting like the past was populated by anything other than real people doing real, often extremely stupid, things.

If you want history that talks to you like an adult, you found your guy.
